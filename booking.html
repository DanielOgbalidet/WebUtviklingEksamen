BOOK TABLE

Warm welcome from us at Gollo cafe! You can book a table for 1 - 5 people. We have no reservations for the tables outside, they are only for drop-in.

If you aren't able to find a table to the right time, no worries! We will hold off a table for drop-in as long as we have the opportunity.
